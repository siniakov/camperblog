About Us

Burr and Den decide to live tiny!

Hello, everyone! We're Burr and Den, and we decided to live tiny while Burr is in grad school. We bought a 1966 Avion that had been sitting neglected in a field for ~30 years, ripped it to pieces and put it back together. We'll be detailing our camper (mis)adventures here - Den was a vanlifer for ~2 years before the Avion; Burr's enthusiastic about tiny living but the Avion's her first foray into living small.
